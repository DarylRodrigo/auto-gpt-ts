Popular UK summer holiday destinations:
1. Scottish Highlands
2. Norfolk
3. Pembrokeshire
4. Fishguard
5. Windsor
6. Cotswolds
7. Yorkshire Dales
8. Hebrides

Findings:
The Scottish Highlands offer wild remote beauty, while Norfolk has beautiful beaches. Pembrokeshire is exceptional for dramatic views, while Fishguard offers Caribbean-like beaches. The Cotswolds and Yorkshire Dales both offer stunning landscapes, while the Hebrides are ideal for a remote getaway.
